About Us

EPM is a professional orchestra performing in the Midlands and north of England. Originally founded in 1998, the orchestra was revived in 2018 and is now Orchestra in Residence at Nottingham Trent University, with whom they have already undertaken a number of exciting ventures.

Snowman Tour

EPM partnered with 'Let's Sing' and local schoolchildren in December 2019 to perform The Snowman Live to excited families across the north of England. Venues included a sold-out Blackpool Tower Ballroom.

NTU Student Concerto Evening

Members of EPM took part in the inaugural Student Concerto Evening in the recently renovated concert hall at Nottingham Trent University. The event provided a valuable platform for students to perform a short piece accompanied by a live orchestra. Students on a scholarship with EPM also had the opportunity to take part in the orchestra alongside experienced professionals.

Madame Butterfly with Heritage Opera

Members of EPM performed in collaboration with Heritage Opera to put on small-scale productions of operas including Puccini's Madame Butterfly.

Spital Arts Quartet Concert

A string quartet formed from members of EPM gave a recital as part of the 2018 Spital Arts Festival.
